DOM Panels over a Zooming User Interface

The page content is transformed as a world surface. Panels live in a sibling layer, so they remain screen-stable, focusable, draggable, stackable, and resizable while the application surface zooms and pans.

ZUI root

CSS variables drive translate and scale on the app surface; pointer math maps screen coordinates to world coordinates.

Panel layer

Panels are web components outside the transformed surface. This avoids zoom interference and keeps UI controls legible.

Plugins

Draggable, resizable, focusable, closable, miniaturizable, rounded, borderless, titled, and pinnable are installed as capabilities.

panel.open("", "Clock", "width=420,height=310,left=730,top=150,plugins=resizable;focusable;stackable;closable;miniaturizable;pinnable;rounded;titled")
Drag the surface background to pan. Use the zoom buttons or mouse wheel to zoom. Drag panels by their captions. Resize the clock from its lower-right corner. Pin toggles whether a panel is anchored to the viewport or the page world.
